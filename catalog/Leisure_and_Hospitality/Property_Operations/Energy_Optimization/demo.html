Act 1 — The Problem
A fully-booked Saturday afternoon.
The chiller runs at full blast — because it always does.
Chillers are the single largest energy consumer in a hotel, representing 40–55% of the total electricity bill. Yet setpoints are set manually, based on experience — the same value for a quiet Tuesday morning as a hot, fully-booked weekend. That consistency is costing money every single day.
The Energy Problem
40–55%
of total hotel electricity consumption is consumed by chiller (HVAC) systems alone — making it the single biggest lever for reducing the energy bill.
The Manual Approach
Experience
Setpoints are adjusted by on-site engineers based on intuition and habit — the same setting for a 40% occupancy cool morning as a 90% occupancy humid afternoon.
