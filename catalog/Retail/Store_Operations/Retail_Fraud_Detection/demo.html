The Challenge

Shrinkage of 1.5–2.5% of revenue leaks through every year — not because the signals aren't there, but because no system joins them up.

01
Fragmented Data Silos
POS, inventory, procurement, and loyalty data live in separate systems. Fraud that spans multiple domains is invisible to any single one.
02
Alert Fatigue
Rules-based alerts flood investigation teams with noise. Most correspond to legitimate activity, eroding trust and capacity to act.
03
Manual Investigation Burden
Teams spend hours gathering context across systems before a case can even be assessed — limiting throughput and response speed.
04
No Behavioural Depth
Individual transactions look legitimate. Systematic misuse only becomes visible across time — something static rules never see.
Why We're Different

This isn't another alert system. It's a continuous intelligence layer that thinks in patterns, not transactions.

●Behavioural Intelligence Over Time
Each agent benchmarks activity against operational peer groups across weeks — not individual events. Systematic drift surfaces before it becomes a confirmed loss.
●Cross-System Signal Aggregation
Five agents share a common intelligence layer. A case that touches POS, inventory, and loyalty simultaneously surfaces automatically — invisible to any single dashboard.
●Autonomous Investigation Summaries
When an anomaly is detected, the agent generates a structured evidence summary ready for review — eliminating hours of manual case preparation.
